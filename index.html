Instructions for Slap Jack

The goal is to win all the cards in the deck and ALSO slap the last Jack!!

When you click "Start SlapJack Game!", the deck is shuffled and evenly dealt to both players.

There are 3 ways to win the middle pile:

1. "Slap" a "Jack" card

2. "Slap" a double (two of the same cards in a row, ie: blue 2, red 2)

3. "Slap" a sandwich (two of the same cards seperated by a differnt card, ie: blue 2, green 8, red 2)

If you slap a card that does not meet one of the above conditions, that top card goes to the other player.

If you run out of cards, you have a chance to stay in the game by slapping a "Jack" only (other conditions do not count in this case).

If your oppenent is out of cards and you slap a Jack, you win!!
